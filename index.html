Brian Taki

Solomon Islands — Kiribati Musician & Cultural Icon

Biography

Brian Taki was a Solomon Islands–born musician of Kiribati heritage whose warm acoustic sound and heartfelt storytelling made him one of the most beloved artists across the Pacific. His music carried the spirit of island life — gentle, emotional, and deeply human.

His songs explored love, longing, family, and cultural pride. With tracks such as Koron, Aubino, Manin, and Tia Kara, he touched the hearts of communities in Kiribati, the Solomon Islands, and the wider diaspora.

Even after his passing, Brian Taki’s legacy continues through the voices of those who sing his songs, the families who remember him, and the communities who honor his contribution to Pacific music.
