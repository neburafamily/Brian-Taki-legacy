About Brian Taki

Brian Taki is a gifted Pacific artist whose music blends the heartfelt soul of Kiribati with the rhythmic island groove of the Solomon Islands. His sound carries the warmth of the ocean, the strength of culture, and the spirit of unity.

🌊 Full Biography (Narrative Form) Brian Taki (date of birth unknown – date of passing unknown) was a Solomon Islands–born musician of Kiribati heritage whose music became a defining voice for Pacific island communities. Raised between two cultures, he developed a sound that blended the emotional storytelling of Kiribati music with the melodic warmth of Solomon Islands acoustic traditions. His songs—simple, heartfelt, and deeply human—spread organically through families, villages, and diaspora communities long before digital platforms existed. When YouTube and SoundCloud became popular, fans began uploading his tracks, preserving his legacy for future generations. Brian’s music often explored themes of love, devotion, and longing, delivered with a sincerity that made listeners feel personally connected to him. Tracks like Koron, Aubino, Manin, and Tia Kara remain staples of Kiribati gatherings and island nostalgia. After his passing, tributes poured in across the Pacific, honoring him as a gifted artist who inspired both Solomon Islanders and I‑Kiribati people. Today, his songs continue to be shared, celebrated, and cherished—keeping his voice alive in the hearts of the communities he touched.
